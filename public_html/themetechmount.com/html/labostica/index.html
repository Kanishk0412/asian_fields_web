About Us

AISANFIELDS POLLUTION CONTROL RESEARCH COUNCIL (APCRC)

APCRC is an incorporated non profit body established for protection of the environment, the prevention of pollution and environmental degradation, the preparation of Environmental Plans for the management and protection of the environment. It is established under the Companies Act 2013, incorporated by Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India and established to plan and execute nation-wide programme for the prevention, control and abetment of environmental pollution as per laid down standards for the quality of environments in its various aspects, development of strategies, technology perspectives, key industrial capabilities to prevent pollution. Having a pan-India presence, APCRC has a dynamic network of 20 laboratories, 30 outreach centres, 3 Innovation Centres and 3 units We are involve in research and analysis on rational management of resources and waste, the impact assessment, pollution control, tackle actions on climate change and halt the risk of loss of species and habitats, while helping to promote green growth within the contents of circular economy, integrated network of waste management installations promoting separation systems at source, as an important target for recycling and recovery.
