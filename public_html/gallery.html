Dhanbad Conclave 2021

National Conclave on pollution control and prevention culture was held in Dhanbad district of Jharkhand in Dec 2021. The program was inaugurated by Shri. Raj Sinha, MLA of Jharkhand and there were around 50 people including guest speakers. Guest speakers were professors from IIT Jharkhand, Environmental officers, environmentalists.

ASIANFIELDS Pollution Control Research Council is established to plan and execute nation-wide programme for the prevention, control and abetment of environmental pollution as per laid down standards for the quality of environments in its various aspects.
